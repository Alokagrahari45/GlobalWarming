How We Can Combat Global Warming

  • Adopt Renewable Energy:
    Solar, wind, and hydroelectric power are cleaner alternatives to fossil fuels and can significantly reduce global emissions.
  • Energy Efficiency:
    Using energy-efficient appliances, lighting, and building designs can reduce energy consumption.
  • Reforestation:
    Planting trees and restoring forests can help absorb CO2 from the atmosphere, acting as carbon sinks.
  • Public Awareness:
    Increasing public knowledge and encouraging sustainable lifestyles through education and policy changes can make a big difference.
